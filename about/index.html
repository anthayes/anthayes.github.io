Ant has over 30 years experience in the IT industry. He has achieved 11 AWS certifications including Solutions Architect Professional, Security Specialty, Machine Learning Specialty and DevOps Engineer Professional.

Since leaving that business in 2013, he has held various positions with large Global System Integrators, Managed Service Providers, Resellers, Services businesses and Cloud Vendors.
